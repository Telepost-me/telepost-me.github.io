# How to start posting through Telepost?

## 1. Create new bot and connect it to Telepost

We prepared [detailed instructions](personal-bot-for-telepost.mdx), where your can find how to do that.

## 2. Add your bot to Administrators of channel

Click on channel's name, go to "Channel Settings", then open "Administrators" and add bot, created at step #1:

![image](/images/106378979-c16a4980-63b9-11eb-9721-3233b19b308d.png)

## 3. Change channel bot in Telepost settings

Open "My channels", then select in list needed channel, see screenshot:

![image](/images/106379002-f6769c00-63b9-11eb-86ff-53510a2fc498.png)

Click on "Change bot", select bot created at step #1 and connect it to Telepost (step #2):

![image](/images/106379045-54a37f00-63ba-11eb-97f7-27f0e7da55cb.png)

Done! Now your can posting through [{{ telepost.bot.name }}]({{ telepost.bot.url }}).

Also we prepared [instructions how to connect your channel](add-channel-in-telepostbot.mdx).
